1. The system shall provide search filters for Countries and job categories, to unemployed users, or type of business, to freelance users, to set search criteria.

2. The system shall provide a list of job offers whenever an unemployed user carries out a research.

3. The system shall provide a set of ordering system criteria for the list of job offers, whenever an unemployed user carries out a research, which must include:
	• Most recent ones,
	• Decreasing salary,
	• Increasing salary,
	• The ones closest to the expiration date.
